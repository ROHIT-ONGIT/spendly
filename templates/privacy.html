Privacy Policy

Effective date: 29 May 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data Spendly collects, how it is used, and how it is protected. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and hashed password when you register
  • Expense records — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually
  • Usage data — basic server logs such as page visits and error reports, used solely for debugging

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate the Service:

  • To authenticate you and display your expense records
  • To generate summaries and reports within your account
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve reliability

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond running Spendly for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secured database accessible only to the application. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using industry-standard algorithms before being saved. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your data is permanently removed.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your financial records are yours alone. The only exception would be if we are required to disclose information by law or a valid legal process — in which case we would notify you to the extent permitted by law.

5. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to deliver the product:

  • Google Fonts — loads typefaces from Google's CDN. Google may log the request. No personal data is shared.

No analytics platforms, advertising networks, or tracking scripts are embedded in Spendly.

6. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie contains no personal information and is deleted when you log out or close your browser. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data associated with your account
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Children's Privacy

Spendly is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out to us at contact.pixelanimations@gmail.com.
